Healthy Foods for High Blood Pressure
-----------------------------------------------------

Aim for foods rich in potassium, magnesium and calcium to lower your blood pressure.


  • Banana

  • Oranges

  • Apricots

  • Potatoes

  • Sweet Potatoes

  • Beets

  • Yogurt and Milk

  • Salmon

  • Dry Fruits
