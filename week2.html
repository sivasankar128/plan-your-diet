Nutrition Plan:
--------------------

Ensure a balanced diet with a mix of proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats. Here's a sample meal plan:

Day 8 :

  • Breakfast: Whole grain waffles with Greek yogurt and berries
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken Caesar salad
  • Snack: Sliced cucumber with hummus
  • Dinner: Baked tilapia with quinoa and steamed green beans

Day 9 :

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and feta cheese
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with a side of mixed nuts
  • Snack: Apple slices with almond butter
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with brown rice and broccoli

Day 10 :

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola and mixed berries
  • Lunch: Chickpea and vegetable stir-fry with quinoa
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with sweet potato and asparagus

Day 11 :

  • Breakfast: Spinach and mushroom omelette with whole grain toast
  • Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens and balsamic vinaigrette
  • Snack: Mixed nuts and dried fruits
  • Dinner: Baked chicken breast with brown rice and roasted Brussels sprouts

Day 12 :

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with chia seeds, sliced banana, and a sprinkle of cinnamon
  • Lunch: Quinoa and black bean salad with avocado dressing
  • Snack: Hummus with carrot sticks
  • Dinner: Grilled salmon with wild rice and steamed asparagus

Day 13 :

  • Breakfast: Cottage cheese pancakes with fresh berries
  • Lunch: Grilled shrimp and quinoa salad with mixed greens
  • Snack: Trail mix with dried fruits and mixed nuts
  • Dinner: Baked cod with mashed sweet potato and saut√©ed spinach

Day 14 :

  • Breakfast: Whole grain pancakes with almond butter and sliced strawberries
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with a side of carrot sticks
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and a sprinkle of granola
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken thighs with wild rice and roasted broccoli

Tips:
-------

  • Warm up before every workout session.
  • Listen to your body and rest if you feel fatigued or in pain.
  • Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep for recovery.
  • Stay consistent with both your workouts and nutrition plan.
  • Consult a fitness professional for personalized advice.
